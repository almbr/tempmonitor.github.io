About us

We are a team of 3 students collaborating in a school project to operate a functional company. The Temp monitor is just a part of the project, but our most beloved one due to our high ambitions and goals with it.

A school project gone too far

This company orginates from a popular Swedish final project concept where students shall organize a group of people to operate a startup company. This concept is hold by "ung företagsamhet".

We chose very early on to work with this togheter due to our high ambitions. We wanted to create a complex product that was easy to use for users. We had an idea early on of a problem we wanted to solve. We had a LAN were we all had issues with our computers and the only solution avalible to this is via software where a large part of the monitor has to be compromised. So we chosed to create a contraption to capture the necessary values to diagnose your computer on a secondary monitor. We also saw an opportunity for home server-users to have such a tool where a montitor isn't avalible.

Made in Sweden

We have developed hardware and software from scratch. If you are interested in our project, you can access our repository at Github. It's completely Open Source and free for use for everybody.

We also manufacture ourselves. Circuit boards, which we design, and components are delivered to us. After that we solder, assemble and program the product.


And so Temp monitor is Made in Sweden .
