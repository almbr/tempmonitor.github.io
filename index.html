Temp monitor

Temp monitor is an open source and hardware monitor to show system resources such as CPU and GPU temperature, fan speeds, RAM usage, GPU and CPU load.

The monitor is connected to your computer via USB and can be customised with its associated program.

Temp monitor is soon to be availible for Windows with planned support for Linux coming up.

We do things ourselves

We have developed both hardware and software from scratch. If you are interested you can access all our work as it is hosted publicly on github. Open Source and free for anyone to use.

We also manufacture ourselves. Circuit boards and components are delivered to us. After that we program, assemble and solder everything.

And so Temp monitor is Made in Sweden.

But why?

We don't like troublesome computers and we don't like bad-looking utility programs that cover areas of the screen.

Therefore we decided to build a solution ourselves.

Temp monitor solves the problem of occupying your computer monitor. Instead you have constant access to the information that you need, without disturbing what you're actually doing.
